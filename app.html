Using OutOfTheGroupChat: A Step-by-Step Guide

Our trip planner app helps you prioritize and plan your trip with your group, saving you time and providing an organized format for all the important details.

User Guide:

  • To navigate to the next page, click the "Next" button.
  • To go back to the previous page, click the "Previous" button.
  • When you reach the end of the planner, press the "Download PDF" button to obtain a copy of your trip plan.
  • Additional instructions can be found on each page to help you use the planner more effectively.
  • Be as specific as possible to help with planning and organization.

Note:

  • Do not reload the page, as this will cause your information to be lost.
  • All sections do not need to be filled out in order to download a PDF of your trip plan.

Happy planning and safe travels!

    Trip ideas

    Safari Adventure in Maasai Mara

    Experience the thrill of wildlife in the famous Maasai Mara Game Reserve.

    • Stay at a tented camp or lodge and enjoy the sounds of the wilderness.
    • Take a hot air balloon ride for an aerial view of the wildlife.
    • Visit a Maasai village and learn about their culture.

    Coastal Getaway

    Relax on the sandy beaches and soak up the sun.

    • Stay in a beautiful villa by the beach with your groups.
    • Enjoy water sports activities such as jet skiing, snorkelling, and diving.
    • Visit historical sites such as Fort Jesus and the Old Town of Mombasa
    • Explore marine life at the Marine National Park in Malindi

    Mount Kenya Hiking Expedition

    Enjoy breathtaking views of the mountain range and surrounding landscape.

    • Trek to the summit of Mount Kenya, the second highest mountain in Africa.
    • Camp along the way and experience the beauty of nature.
    • Visit the nearby Ol Pejeta Conservancy and see endangered wildlife like rhinos and chimpanzees.
