Using OutOfTheGroupChat:
A Step-by-Step Guide

User Guide:

  • Click "Start" to begin.
  • To navigate to the next page, click the "Next" button.
  • To go back to the previous page, click the "Previous" button.
  • When you reach the end of the planner, press the "Download PDF" button to obtain a copy of your trip plan.
  • Additional instructions can be found on each page to help you use the planner more effectively.
  • Be as specific as possible to help with planning and organization.

Note:

  • Do not reload the page, as this will cause your information to be lost.
  • All sections do not need to be filled out in order to download a PDF of your trip plan.

Happy planning and safe travels!

    Safari Adventure in Maasai Mara

    Experience the thrill of wildlife in the famous Maasai Mara Game Reserve.

    • Stay at a Tented Camp or Lodge and enjoy the sounds of the wilderness. Options include high-end luxury camps or more affordable, rustic options.
    • Take a Hot Air Balloon Ride for an aerial view of the wildlife. This is an unforgettable way to experience the vast savannahs and wildlife in the area.
    • Visit a Maasai VillageMaasai village and learn about their culture. Get an authentic cultural experience by interacting with the Maasai people and learning about their customs and way of life.
    • Witness the Great Wildebeest Migration , one of the most incredible wildlife spectacles on earth. This annual event sees millions of wildebeest, zebras, and gazelles migrating across the Mara in search of fresh grazing.

    Coastal Getaway

    Relax on the sandy beaches and soak up the sun.

    • Stay in a Beautiful Villa by the beach with your group, complete with private pool and ocean views.
    • Enjoy Water Sports activities such as jet skiing, snorkelling, and diving. The coastal waters are teeming with colourful marine life, including dolphins and sea turtles.
    • Visit Historical Sites such as Fort Jesus and the Old Town of Mombasa. These ancient landmarks are a testament to the area's rich history and multicultural influences.
    • Explore marine life at the Marine National Park in Malindi. This protected marine reserve is home to over 600 species of fish and is a great spot for snorkeling or scuba diving.
    • Experience the Local Cuisine by trying seafood delicacies such as Swahili fish curry and grilled prawns.

    Mount Kenya Hiking Expedition

    Enjoy breathtaking views of the mountain range and surrounding landscape.

    • Trek to the Summit of Mount Kenya, the second highest mountain in Africa. This challenging but rewarding climb takes you through diverse landscapes, from equatorial forests to alpine moorlands.
    • Camp along the way and experience the beauty of nature. Enjoy the serenity of the mountain wilderness and sleep under the stars.
    • Visit the nearby Ol Pejeta Conservancy and see endangered wildlife like rhinos and chimpanzees. This wildlife conservancy is home to several endangered species and is a great place for a game drive or walking safari.
    • Relax at the end of your hike with a stay at a Luxury Lodge, complete with spa treatments and gourmet dining.
